Stay at the Condado Vanderbilt

The Condado Vanderbilt Hotel is an iconic luxury destination in San Juan, Puerto Rico. With breathtaking oceanfront views, exquisitely designed rooms, and world-class amenities, the Vanderbilt offers guests an unforgettable experience rooted in history, elegance, and comfort.

Whether you're traveling for romance, business, or relaxation, this five-star hotel features fine dining restaurants, full-service spa facilities, and a stunning infinity pool overlooking the Atlantic Ocean. Its prime location in the trendy Condado district places you just minutes away from Old San Juan, vibrant nightlife, and upscale shopping.
