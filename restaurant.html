Dine at Marmalade

Marmalade offers a world-class fine dining experience with a Caribbean twist. Tucked away in the charming streets of Old San Juan, this upscale restaurant is known for its creative tasting menus, extensive wine selection, and romantic ambiance that makes it perfect for special occasions.

The restaurant’s modern interior features soft lighting, cozy seating, and artistic decor, while the culinary offerings include seasonal ingredients blended into memorable dishes. Whether you’re savoring their famous white bean soup or exploring wine pairings from their curated cellar, Marmalade delivers an experience that's both refined and unforgettable.
